## Scenario Guidance
Indikin operates under three potential postures:
- **Lean**: $35K–$40K monthly burn (Runway protection).
- **Base (Recommended)**: ~$52.3K monthly burn (Balanced scale).
- **Aggressive**: $75K–$90K monthly burn (Market land-grab).
