# Strategic Intelligence (.brain)

This domain houses the business logic, market positioning, and strategic roadmaps for the Indikin ecosystem. It represents the "intellectual core" of the project's migration to Ethereum.

---

## Domain Structure

### 📁 [strategy/](./strategy/)
Contains the long-term vision and operational roadmaps.
- **Business Plan**: The canonical internal draft for the $10M Hybrid Round.
- **Roadmap 2026**: Quarterly milestones for migration, onboarding, and revenue scale.
- **Acquisitions Map**: Targets for the Film3 infrastructure roll-up.

### 📁 [tokenomics/](./tokenomics/)
The economic engine of the Indikin index.
- **Tokenomics Brief**: Core bonding mechanics and deflationary burn logic.
- **Financial Projections**: Yield forecasts based on ecosystem volume.

### 📁 [go-to-market/](./go-to-market/)
Growth playbooks for all ecosystem participants.
- **Filmmakers**: Personal acquisition and ONBA-assisted onboarding.
- **Fans & Consumers**: IndiFlix engagement and "Star Pass" value props.
- **Liquidity Providers**: DeFi yield and index stability strategies.

### 📁 [pitch-decks/](./pitch-decks/)
Visual assets for fundraising and community alignment.
- **Ethereum Seed Deck**: The primary scale-round presentation.

### 📁 [team/](./team/)
Profiles of the founding team infrastructure.
- CVs for **Michael Parenti** (CEO), **Ahmed Kallel** (CTO), **Rich Tella** (COO), and **Regan Milne** (AI Infrastructure).
