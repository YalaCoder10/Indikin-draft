## Segment: Liquidity Providers
**Internal Draft · March 2026 · Confidential**

---

## Segment Overview

Liquidity providers are the financial foundation of the Indikin ecosystem. Without deep, stable liquidity in the $INDIKIN/ETH pool on Ethereum, the token index cannot function, yield cannot be generated, and the filmmaker economy cannot operate. This segment must be activated first — before filmmaker onboarding scales and before consumer acquisition begins.

The target profile is crypto-native capital: DeFi-experienced individuals and funds who understand AMM mechanics, are comfortable with token-based yield, and are actively looking for volume-backed returns with asymmetric upside. This is not a retail audience. It is a specific, reachable community with known gathering points, known influencers, and known decision criteria.

## The Value Proposition

### Why a liquidity provider chooses $INDIKIN

| What they get | How it works |
|---|---|
| **Volume-backed yield** | 1% transaction tax on all $INDIKIN trades distributed proportionally to circulating holders — no emissions, no rebasing, real yield |
| **Asymmetric upside** | Early liquidity in a pre-scale ecosystem with a credible Ethereum migration and a $10M raise behind it |
| **Scarcity mechanics** | Treasury holds majority supply and is excluded from rewards — circulating holders capture a disproportionate share of yield |
| **Deflationary pressure** | 0.1% of $INDIKIN supply burned for every new filmmaker onboarded — supply shrinks as ecosystem grows |
| **Real-world asset backing** | Yield is tied to a growing ecosystem of actual film productions, streaming revenues, and filmmaker communities — not speculative tokenomics |
| **DeFi-native mechanics** | Time-locked multi-sig governance, on-chain transparency, no arbitrary minting — built for DeFi-native investor expectations |

- ETH holders seeking diversified yield beyond staking

## Messaging Framework

### Core narrative
*Real yield from real creative output. The Indikin Token Index gives DeFi investors exposure to a growing ecosystem of independent filmmakers — with volume-backed yield, no emissions, and deflationary mechanics that strengthen with every filmmaker who joins.*

### Key messages by objection

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "Why would film generate DeFi-relevant volume?" | Filmmaker token communities drive trading. As each filmmaker grows their audience, their token trades — every trade flows back to $INDIKIN holders. 16 filmmakers, 53 productions already. |
| "This sounds like a memecoin" | No emissions, no rebasing, time-locked multi-sig treasury, on-chain governance. The mechanics are DeFi-native and auditable. |
| "What if volume doesn't materialise?" | Early-phase yield is modest (~2–4% APY). The asymmetric upside is in ecosystem growth. We're not promising 100% APY on day one. |
| "Who is the team?" | Four co-founders at the intersection of Film, DeFi, and AI. Live platform. PulseChain proof of concept. $33K production fund built without external capital. |
| "What's the legal structure?" | Cayman Foundation for token/SAFT; Bitjoin AB for operations. Full legal documentation provided to serious investors before commitment. |
