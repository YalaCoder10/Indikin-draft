## Seed raise — $1M liquidity commitment

The entire $1M raise is deployed as on-chain protocol liquidity across three milestone-gated phases. No capital is allocated to salaries, overheads, or discretionary spend. This is the primary commitment signal to seed investors.

| Phase | Period | Amount | Trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Months 1–6 | **$300,000** | Launch |
| 2 | Months 7–18 | **$400,000** | Volume milestone |
| 3 | Months 19–36 | **$300,000** | Tax escalation |

Phase 2 and 3 inject only when volume thresholds are sustained — protecting capital and demonstrating discipline.

## Tax escalation — pre-committed

The transaction tax increases 1% per year. This is pre-committed at raise — APY improvement is structural, not discretionary. UNI rewards distributed to circulating holders only. Treasury excluded permanently.

| Year | Tax | Avg daily vol (base) | Annual rewards | Est. APY |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1% | $10,000 | ~$36,500 | **~37%** |
| 2 | 2% | $18,000 | ~$131,400 | **~131%** |

APY anchored to $100k circulating cap (1M tokens at $0.10). Always present alongside the volume assumption that produces it. High APY is a function of tight circulating supply — not speculation.
